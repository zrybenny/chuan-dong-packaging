Dongguan Chuan Dong Packaging Products Co., Ltd.

Established in 2015, Dongguan Chuan Dong Packaging Products Co., Ltd. is located in Liaobu Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province. We specialize in the production of high-quality packaging products, including plastic bags, adhesive products, and paper products. Our factory is equipped with a full production line and modern machinery to meet global standards. We are dedicated to providing custom packaging solutions for various industries, ensuring efficient production and premium quality.
